Glossary

Terms:


Bobcat
A medium-sized, furry mammal that can eat children.
Cat
A small, furry mammal that meows.
Dog
A small, furry mammal that barks.
Fish
A scaly aquatic animal that swims in water.
Flamingo
A pink bird.
Gnat
A small flying insect.
Llama
A shaggy-haired beast of burden with a long neck.
Mouse
A tiny mammal.
Quail
A small bird.
Rhino
A scaly mammal.
Swan
A white bird.
Toucan
A colorful bird.
Whale
A huge aquatic mammal.
Yak
A shaggy-haired beast of burden.
Zebra
Sort of a horse with white/black stripes.
